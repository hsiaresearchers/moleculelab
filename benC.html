Dr. Ben Chin-Yee

Role: Dr. Benjamin Chin-Yee is a hematologist in the Division of Hematology at Western University, Canada. As a clinician-researcher, he is the principal investigator of many of our lab's projects...

Education: MD and MA from University of Toronto. PhD candidate and Gates Scholar in History and Philosophy of Science at the University of Cambridge.

Research Interests

  • Dr. Chin-Yee does interdisciplinary research that spans a range of fields from bioethics to the history and philosophy of medicine.
    He is particularly interested in the impact of technology on the patient-physician relationship and how to integrate values and evidence to improve clinical decision-making. His current work focuses on the impact of big data, genomic medicine and artificial intelligence in clinical practice.
  • Dr. Chin-Yee is currently accepting students/trainees interested in research projects in hematology, philosophy, medical communication, and bioethics.
