Publications

Selected research outputs from the Chin-Yee & Hsia Hematology Research Lab

The Chin-Yee & Hsia Hematology Research Lab is dedicated to advancing the understanding, diagnosis, and treatment of blood disorders through rigorous clinical and translational research. Below is a selection of highlights showcasing our impact, followed by comprehensive publication lists for Dr. Benjamin Chin-Yee and Dr. Cyrus Hsia.

Selected Highlights

  • Cancer Labeling, Risk Perception, and Treatment Choices in Clonal Cytopenia of Undetermined Significance. JAMA Network Open. 2025;8(7):e2523733. (Chin-Yee B, Latham AJ, Varga S)
  • SGLT2i-associated erythrocytosis: Closing the evidence gap. Blood Advances. 2025;9(16):4321-4322. (Chin-Yee B; Hsia CC)
  • A Single-Center Retrospective Study of the Investigation of Monoclonal B-Cell Lymphocytosis (MBL) and Chronic Lymphocytic Leukemia (CLL) in Southwestern Ontario: Are We Over-Investigating? Int J Lab Hematol. 2025;47(5):891-897. (Hoxha O; Lad M; Hedley BD; Chin-Yee B; Hsia CC; et al.)
  • Eltrombopag versus intravenous immunoglobulin for first-line treatment of immune thrombocytopenia: a randomized trial. Lancet Haematol. 2020;7(9):e640-e648. (Hsia CC, co-investigator)
  • Erythrocytosis associated with sodium–glucose cotransporter 2 inhibitors: case series and literature review. CMAJ. 2020;192(7):E171-E175. (Chin-Yee B, first author)
  • Next-generation sequencing panels for hematologic malignancies: implementation and clinical impact. Eur J Haematol. 2019;103(6):599-606. (Chin-Yee B, first author)
