Dr. Cyrus Hsia

Role: Hematologist, Principal Investigator

Education: Dr. Hsia completed his undergraduate degrees in Mathematics and Human Biology at the University of Toronto. He attended medical school at the University of Western Ontario (Schulich School of Medicine & Dentistry) and completed his Internal Medicine residency and fellowship training in Hematology at UWO as well. Today, he is an associate professor at Western University and associate medical director of the blood transfusion laboratory at the London Health Sciences Centre (LHSC). Dr. Hsia’s research interests are in general hematology and transfusion medicine. Mainly in the safe and appropriate utilization of blood and blood products as it relates to hematologic diseases. Specific clinical areas of interest include ITP, MDS, iron deficiency and overload. Dr. Hsia has worked extensively with students, facilitating research projects and student advancement.
