From Bench to Bedside: Transforming Hematology Together

About Team Publications Get Involved

At the Chin-Yee & Hsia Hematology Research Lab, we are committed to bridging insights between the clinical laboratory and the bedside to transform care for patients with blood disorders. Our research focuses on improving the early identification of rare hematologic diseases, and studying how novel biomarkers and AI-driven tools can guide real-world clinical decision-making. We are also investigating how clonal evolution in conditions like paroxysmal nocturnal hemoglobinuria (PNH) shapes disease trajectories over time — not just through numbers, but by linking longitudinal lab findings with patient outcomes. Central to this work is improving communication between the laboratory and clinical teams, ensuring that new evidence, diagnostic reports, and biomarkers are shared clearly and translated into timely, evidence-based decisions for patients. We are equally committed to training the next generation of hematologists and physician-scientists. Students in our lab gain hands-on experience in projects that directly connect research with patient care — within a collaborative environment where curiosity drives discovery and discovery improves lives.
