About the Lab

Advancing hematology through translational research and patient-centered care.

Our Mission

The Chin-Yee & Hsia Hematology Research Lab is dedicated to advancing the understanding, diagnosis, and treatment of blood disorders through rigorous clinical and translational research. We work at the intersection of laboratory science and clinical practice to ensure that discoveries translate into meaningful improvements in patient care.

About This Team

Bridging the Clinical Lab and the Bedside

At the Chin-Yee & Hsia Hematology Research Lab, we connect laboratory insights with real-world clinical decisions to transform care for patients with blood disorders.

Our mission is to integrate translational science with compassionate clinical practice, ensuring that discoveries at the bench make a meaningful impact at the bedside.

Lab ↔ Clinic
  • Rare diseases focus: PNH, immune cytopenias, unexplained cytopenias
  • Biomarkers & AI: predictive models to sharpen decisions
  • Longitudinal datasets: linking lab signals to outcomes
  • Training: hands-on mentorship for students & fellows

“We bridge insights between the clinical laboratory and the bedside to transform patient care.”

— Lab Mission

Our Focus

We study blood disorders such as paroxysmal nocturnal hemoglobinuria (PNH), immune cytopenias, and unexplained cytopenias. Our work emphasizes connecting laboratory signals with real patient outcomes through robust datasets and AI-driven prediction models.

Our People

Our team includes hematologists, data scientists, trainees, and collaborators who share a passion for advancing both scientific understanding and patient care.

Our Core Values

  • Innovation: Developing new diagnostic and therapeutic strategies.
  • Collaboration: Building strong partnerships across disciplines.
  • Compassion: Keeping patients at the center of all we do.
  • Education: Training the next generation of hematology leaders.
